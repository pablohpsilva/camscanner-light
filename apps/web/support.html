Frequently asked questions

Does it work offline?

Yes. ScannerCam Light does everything on your device and needs no internet connection.

Where are my documents stored?

On your phone only. Image files are saved in the app's storage and indexed in a local database — nothing is uploaded.

How do I export a PDF?

Open a document, choose export, and use the system share sheet to save or send the PDF anywhere.

Is it free?

Yes, it's free with no ads. If you'd like to support development, you can donate below or use the optional donation link inside the app.

How do I free up space?

Delete documents you no longer need from the library; their image files are removed from your device.
