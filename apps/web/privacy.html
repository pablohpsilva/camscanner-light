Privacy

Privacy Policy

ScannerCam Light is built to keep your documents private. In short: everything stays on your device.

Effective date: 6 July 2026

What we collect

Nothing. ScannerCam Light does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. There are no user accounts and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Where your data lives

  • Scanned images are stored as files on your device only.
  • Document titles and text are kept in a local database on your device.
  • Text recognition (OCR) runs entirely on your device — no images or text are sent anywhere.

Network access

The Android version requests no INTERNET permission and makes no network requests. The only time an external destination can open is when you deliberately tap the optional donation link, which opens your browser to a third‑party donation page. We don't control or receive data from that page.

Sharing

When you export or share a document, you choose the destination through your device's own share sheet. That action, and where the file goes, is entirely under your control.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data, it poses no data-collection risk to users of any age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
